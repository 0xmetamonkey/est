## üéØ How to Use

### Starting a Recording Session:

1. **Start any shot** with "Capture Frame" enabled
   - Examples: "Shoot 1kin1k", "Self tape", "Sketch/paint"

2. **Tap the camera button** (üì∏) during your shot

3. **Camera screen opens** with:
   - Live camera preview
   - Your shot title at the top
   - Recording controls at the bottom

4. **Choose your action**:
   - **Take a photo**: Tap the camera icon (left)
   - **Record video**: Tap the large center button
   - **Switch camera**: Tap the flip icon (top right)

### Recording Video:

1. Tap the **large white circle** to start recording
2. Button turns **red** and shows **REC** indicator
3. Timer counts up: REC 00:15, REC 01:30, etc.
4. Tap again to **stop recording**
5. Video automatically saved
6. Green notification shows duration

### Taking Photos:

1. Tap the **camera icon** (left button)
2. Photo captured instantly
3. Saved as a "frame"
4. Counter increments
5. Purple notification confirms

### Switching Cameras:

1. Tap the **flip icon** (top right)
2. Switches between front/back camera
3. Perfect for selfie-style content or filming your work
