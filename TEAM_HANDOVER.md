## ğŸ¯ What Is This Project?

**Shot by Shot** (aka "Enjoy Super Time") helps you **film your life** instead of tracking productivity.

### Core Philosophy
- Your day = 6 films (4 hours each)
- Each film has scenes ("shots") you choose to capture
- Timer shows time **flowing**, not running out
- Camera/recording makes mundane moments filmable

**NOT:** A productivity tracker  
**IS:** A life documentation tool
